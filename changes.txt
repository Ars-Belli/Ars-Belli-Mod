
# Fort Limit:

base limit of 5
fort techs give flat 3
country ranks give no fort limit
cities give no fort limit
locations give 0.05 per location
nobility power no longer gives fort limit
defensiveness gives half as much % fort limit (25%)
defensiveness no longer gives a combat speed bonus
defensiveness no longer gives an Army Movement Speed Penalty
defensiveness gives a scaling -10% siege ability instead
offensiveness gives half or less as much defensiveness% penalty (20%?)
offensiveness no longer gives Army Movement Speed
Offensiveness grants a scaling 25% Logistics Distance instead
Offensiveness grants 20% assault ability
Offensiveness grants 20% siege ability
over fort limit gives -1% fort defense for every percentage point over limit, capped at -50%

# Crusades:

valid crusade targets are now any heathen kingdom/empire whose capital lies in Europe, North Africa, or the Middle East (in addition to vanilla holders of Catholic holy sites)
crusades and jihads now have a global cooldown of 100 years (one per religion)

# Personal Unions:

junior partners (and any member of a federal union) get a "Force Break Union" button in the union panel that generates a random new ruler for them, instantly breaking the union; costs 20 legitimacy and has a 50-year cooldown

# Access Diplomacy:

new "Pay for X" diplomatic actions (military access, fleet basing, trade access, food access, fondaco rights) with a gold slider so the player can sweeten the offer; AI acceptance scales with offered gold
"Pay for Military Access" and "Pay for Fleet Basing Rights" auto-accept unless the AI is a rival (rivalry overrides any gold offered); the other three still require enough gold to convince the AI

# Guarantees:

guarantees are now two-step: GP/major uses "Propose Guarantee", recipient must accept or reject before the guarantee activates (vanilla one-click "Offer Guarantee" is hidden)
rejecting a proposed guarantee just clears the offer, no opinion penalty
new "Break Others' Guarantee" action lets you forcibly remove a guarantee placed on you (requires peace, creates a truce on use)
guarantee relation no longer grants +50 opinion

# Opinion:

new "Worsen Opinion" action: worsens YOUR opinion of another country by -100, decays over 10 years

# Power Ranking & Diplo Limits:

click the country rank label in the top bar to open a tier-list panel listing every country in each rank (GP / Major / Normal / Small / Minor) with flag, name, and power score
the foreign country diplomacy panel now shows the viewed country's MP rank, power score, and AP/DP/GP usage and limits
a country in both an alliance and a defensive league with you counts only once (defensive points win); personal unions count as defensive but don't double-count if already in a defensive league
only GPs and Majors get guarantee points; everyone else gets 0
mutable alert when over the alliance limit
UI overflow fixes for the own/foreign points display

# Pops & Setup:

updated for game 1.2
pop adjustments across Asia: Timurid, Delhi, Khmer, Ayutthaya, Ming (middle kingdom), and the Lordship of Pale

# Patch Compatibility:

Unconditional Surrender button removed (base game now introduces one)
Enforce Peace warning removed (base game now asks both defender and attacker)
