[h1]Ars Belli Multiplayer  Mod[/h1]

[i]A gameplay-focused mod for Europa Universalis V, built primarily for multiplayer sessions.[/i]

Ars Belli adds a custom power-ranking system, diplomatic web limits, and an extensive military/diplomacy rebalance. Supports game version 1.2.*.
Server link: https://discord.gg/ZxbBhu9W

[hr][/hr]

[h2]Multiplayer Power Ranking[/h2]
[list]
[*]Every country is classified into a tier: [b]Great Power, Major Power, Normal, Small, Minor[/b].
[*]A country's "Power Score" is calculated from population, economy, manpower & military strength, navy, and controlled/vassal population.
[*]The score is smoothed over a 12-month target so rankings don't swing wildly month to month; tooltips show the projected score.
[*]Click the country rank label in the top bar to open a tier-list panel listing every country in each rank with flag, name, and power score.
[*]The foreign country diplomacy panel shows the viewed country's MP rank, power score, and diplomatic point usage/limits.
[*]The number of Great Powers and Major Powers is set by game rules at game start.
[/list]

[h2]Diplomatic Limits[/h2]
[list]
[*]Alliance Points (AP) and Defensive Points (DP) cap how tangled the diplomatic web can get; exceeding a limit applies penalties.
[*]A country that is in both an alliance and a defensive league with you counts only once — defensive points take priority.
[*]Personal unions count as defensive, but don't double-count if the partner is already in a defensive league with you.
[*]Only Great Powers and Majors get Guarantee Points; everyone else gets 0.
[*]Mutable alert when you are over the alliance limit, plus UI overflow fixes for the points displays.
[/list]

[h2]Diplomacy Changes[/h2]
[list]
[*][b]Two-step Guarantees:[/b] a GP/Major uses "Propose Guarantee" and the recipient must Accept or Reject before it activates. The old one-click "Offer Guarantee" is hidden. Rejecting just clears the offer — no opinion penalty.
[*][b]Break Others' Guarantee:[/b] forcibly remove a guarantee placed on you. Requires peace and creates a truce on use.
[*]The guarantee relation no longer grants +50 opinion.
[*][b]Worsen Opinion:[/b] new action that worsens YOUR opinion of another country by -100, decaying over 10 years.
[*][b]Pay for Access:[/b] new "Pay for X" actions (military access, fleet basing, trade access, food access, fondaco rights) with a gold slider to sweeten the offer. AI acceptance scales with the gold offered.
[*]"Pay for Military Access" and "Pay for Fleet Basing Rights" auto-accept unless the AI is your rival (rivalry overrides any gold offer). The other three still need enough gold to convince the AI.
[/list]

[h2]Personal Unions[/h2]
[list]
[*][b]Force Break Union:[/b] junior partners — and any member of a federal union — get a button in the union panel that generates a random new ruler and instantly breaks the union. Costs 20 legitimacy and has a 50-year cooldown.
[/list]

[h2]War & Military Rebalancing[/h2]
[list]
[*][b]Ticking warscore[/b] capped at 36 (down from 50), since occupations now give double warscore.
[/list]

[h3]Fort Rebalancing[/h3]
[list]
[*][b]Fort limit:[/b] base limit of 5. Fort techs give a flat +3; locations give +0.05 each. Country rank, city count, and nobility power no longer grant fort limit.
[*][b]Defensiveness:[/b] gives half as much % fort limit (25%). No longer gives a combat speed bonus or an army movement speed penalty — instead grants a scaling -10% siege ability.
[*][b]Offensiveness:[/b] gives half (or less) as much defensiveness penalty (~20%). No longer grants army movement speed — instead grants a scaling 25% logistics distance, +20% assault ability, and +20% siege ability.
[*]Being over your fort limit gives -1% fort defense per percentage point over, capped at -50%.
[/list]

[h2]Crusades & Jihads[/h2]
[list]
[*]Valid crusade targets are now any heathen kingdom/empire whose capital lies in Europe, North Africa, or the Middle East — in addition to vanilla holders of Catholic holy sites.
[*]Crusades and Jihads now share a global cooldown of 100 years (one per religion).
[/list]

[h2]Pops & Setup[/h2]
[list]
[*]Updated for game version 1.2.
[*]Pop adjustments across Asia: Timurid, Delhi, Khmer, Ayutthaya, Ming (Middle Kingdom), and the Lordship of Pale.
[/list]

[h2]Patch Compatibility[/h2]
[list]
[*]Unconditional Surrender button removed (the base game now introduces one).
[*]Enforce Peace warning removed (the base game now asks both defender and attacker to accept).
[/list]
